TeamPerform

TeamPerform automates KPI management and allows you to:

Set goals (and KPIs) centrally for employees at all levels in the organization

Collect data on KPIs in a timely manner

Have regular performance review sessions

Automatically calculate employee bonuses based on their individual achievements

Managing people's performance using KPIs and OKRs is the key to getting great business results and long-term success for the company. The main steps in objectives management are setting goals, measuring performance, and rewarding employees based on their results.

With the TeamPerform system, you can

OKR

can manage your goals and tasks using the OKR (Objectives and Key Results) method:
· to set big goals and define clear criteria for achieving them;
· to build a pyramid of goals, going from the bottom up;
· to create a system for monitoring everything from start to finish.

KPI

can create a balanced scorecard to link your company's strategy with your daily activities; delegate responsibility for goals to your employees using KPIs (Key Performance Indicators).

Formation of motivation cards by employee functions

Make the bonus calculation process more transparent for employees. Transfer the formulas for calculating bonuses from Excel into the automated solution TeamPerform. Replace the complicated rules for performance evaluation and bonus calculation with visual goal maps, which can be customized for each individual employee, position, or department.

You no longer have to fill out key performance indicators (KPIs) in Excel spreadsheets. The calculation of indicators and bonuses for hundreds and thousands of employees will be performed automatically — in one window of the system on the 1C platform.
An employee sees their tasks and can report on their completion in their personal account with a few clicks.
The manager, in turn, can check, change, add new tasks and evaluate according to checklists, regulations, etc.

Motivational goal maps will no longer raise questions from your employees, as they will become intuitive.

Detailing bonus indicators for employees

Adjust the level of detail in the indicators used to calculate bonuses. As an employee, you will be able to see exactly what your bonus is based on and where the planned and actual values come from.

You will understand what is expected of you and what results you have achieved.

As a manager, you will see the results of your team's work with visualization of indicators in the form of dashboards and speedometers.

Employee performance can be assessed using both quantitative and qualitative measures.

The system can be customized to meet the specific needs of your organization through flexible settings. You can define the scope of responsibility for each team member and track performance at the individual, team, departmental, and organizational levels.

Convenient communication with candidates

When the number of employees grows, manual approval processes become too long, involve a large number of participants and require a lot of time and attention from managers. Inevitably, the risk of errors and delays in calculating premiums increases.

Streamline and unify the flow of approvals for managers and employees.

Set a deadline for each step, and ensure transparent control of performance management processes for HR administrators.
Unload your HR department!

Hand over the boring process of admin to the system TeamPerform. The system will guide your employees through the process of approving goals and tracking results. It will tell them what to do and when to do it. They'll get reminders and tips via the system and email.
Facts can be inputted into the system either automatically or through specific forms. The employee only works with the data they need, nothing extra.
The salary is always paid on time!

Everything is calculated automatically for bonuses and indicators, and reports come out with the click of a button.

Providing the OKR methodology

While implementing the company's strategy, ensure effective control over the set goals and tasks based on the principles of the Objectives and Key Results methodology, such as accessibility of key indicators, bottom-up setting, flexibility, and etc.

A map of the company's goals according to the BSC methodology

Create balanced goal maps that allow you to coordinate operational activities with the implementation of the company's strategy by allocating and delegating responsibility to specific employees

Measurable indicators (KPIs)

Monitor the business activity and effectiveness of key indicators (financial, production, etc.) of each employee and any structural unit

SMART tasks

Manage initiatives, tasks and projects according to SMART criteria

Standards and checklist

A convenient tool for evaluating staff compliance with internal procedures and regulations

Unlimited library of indicators

Maintain a united library of target indicators and group them according to your methodology. There are no quantity limits

Maintaining change history

Changes to the system are saved, allowing you to view the history of your work with interactive objects, or to return to a previous version if an error occurs

Corporate indicators

The ability to create and maintain several types of indicators: corporate, team, individual

Goal maps (kpi matrices)

A goal map for an employee

Create a matrix of key performance indicators for each employee, so that their performance can be monitored and their bonus calculated

Interface (Widget) Settings

Set up the visual display of your desktop using a flexible widget system

Formula Constructor

Flexible configuration of algorithms for calculating the actual values of indicators in user mode

Groupings by KPI type

Grouping of target indicators by type: corporate, team, individual.

Persons responsible for data entry

The ability to appoint persons responsible for making a plan and a fact according to indicators

Mass input of planned and actual values

Use a special proforma for mass data input of plans and facts

Cascading of planned and fact values

Collect data on planned and fact values from subordinates to the head or vice versa in real time

Maintaining the periodicity of the goal map

Create maps for different periods: for a day, a week, a month, a quarter, a half-year, a year

Aggregation of planned and fact values

Creating a cascading system of indicators. Values of plans and actual data will be collected from month to quarter, and so on

Maintaining analytical slices

Collect data on the plan and the fact for various cross-sections within the same indicator

Goal Map Templates

Create templates and apply them to employees with the same set of metrics

Extended role model of the system

The basic roles of the system are: HR administrator of the organization, HR Officer, Manager, User, Salary Calculator, Division bonus calculation, Project Manager, Administrator. In addition, roles can be defined dynamically depending on the position in the organizational structure

Tasks and projects

Control and execution of tasks

The system allows an employee to view their tasks and deadlines, criteria, priorities for its implementation, as well as attach any reports and documents

Maintaining Delegation function

Any task assigned to an employee or manager for a certain period can be splitted into separate subtasks for the final performers (subordinates). The system provides the ability to view the hierarchy of delegated tasks

Meeting management

The ability of forming minutes of meetings and distributing operational tasks to their participants

Notification system

Maintaining employee notifications about tasks. Notifications are delivered by the 1C: Enterprise platform or by e-mail

The history of working on the task

The change log, which records any actions when working on the task. The history of changes for each task is available at any time

Project management function

Create milestones, stages, and sub-stages for the project - assign tasks based on them and monitor their progress. The project's tasks are displayed on a visual Gantt chart

Evaluation of tasks based on the SMART principle

Evaluate the assigned tasks according to SMART criteria or according to the yes/no principle

Correlation between task completion and remuneration

The ratio between completed tasks and approved ones (in accordance with their weight) is calculated as a performance coefficient and is used to determine the amount of employee remuneration

Evaluation by checklists and standards

Evaluation scale

The system allows you to use the classical behavioral scale (BARS), ABC, and an arbitrary scale - and assign the necessary weight for the evaluation standard

Comments on the evaluation

The system implements the ability to add comments to each evaluation, which provides regular feedback for experts and evaluated employees

Mass input of results of evaluation

With the help of a panel of standards, the evaluating employee can put down grades in one click

Self-evaluation

The employee can conduct a self-evaluation according to the established criteria, which allows to identify the degree of understanding of the imposed requirements by the employee

Impact on remuneration

The program, in accordance with the specified algorithm, transforms grades into numerical values, which are later used to calculate remuneration

Organizational design and business processes

Formation of the organizational structure of the company

Building a management structure for the connection between managers and subordinates, hierarchy at the level of departments and employees in them

Grading employees

Determination of grades for categories of employees with using grades in a remuneration system and automated calculations

Process of coordinating goals and KPIs

The "Planning" business process is necessary to coordinate the work of users in the system when they are filling out goal cards, which are then checked by the supervisor and approved by the relevant person

The performance evaluation process (by KPI)

The business process "Summarizing" is needed when closing the reporting period, entering and approving the actual values, calculating the premium

Organizational change processes

The processes of approving new units based on a specific scenario (including approving the expense budget for new units) and the elimination of existing units

Visualization of the organizational design process

Displaying in the structure of staff positions that have not yet been approved

Setting up the assignment of business process tasks

The ability to assign employees to different stages of a business process

Business Process Constructor

Advanced functionality for creating your own business processes other than those installed in the system

Task Reminders

Automatic reminders about tasks that need to be completed on running processes

Control over the execution of business process tasks

The system allows you to use a special report to monitor employees and running business processes, and track statuses

Calculation of premiums

Payment based on the result of KPI fulfillment

In the system, it is possible to automate the calculation of the bonus part of the employee's remuneration based on the achieved result

The use of all types of evaluation in the calculation of remuneration

Target indicators, SMART tasks, Standards - it is possible to use all types of evaluation when calculating the final employee bonus

Formulas for calculating bonuses

With the help of a flexible formula editor, flexible bonus algorithms are configured in user mode. Quantitative and qualitative performance indicators are used when setting up formulas

Bonuses for various periods

Bonus transparency

Secure access to data

Budgeting of the payroll according to planned accruals

Strategy according to the BSC methodology

Building a BSC map

The indicator system

Automatic building of the indicator panel based on the balanced goal map

Decomposition of goals

Thanks to the Drill Down technique, an employee can move from the upper level of goal grouping to the lower level of the target indicators of the responsible employee

Custom BSC card structure

Create your own perspectives, groups of goals, determine their balance and interrelation

Information layers

Using the capabilities of the BSC map, you can switch to other information layers to determine the relationship between the goals of individual employees and departments and the strategic goals of the organization

KPI monitoring, multidimensional analysis

Dashboards of business indicators, KPIs

The system includes a graphical visualization tool for the most important indicators for monitoring and management in a generalized, enlarged form

A great reporting tool

The analytical capabilities of the program allow you to present data in various ways: in a static form, in dynamics, in the form of structured tabular reports, and in the form of diagrams and graphs

Popular formats for uploading reports

The ability for a manager to upload and analyze data in the form of a report on his subordinates. Uploading reports in Excel, Word, PDF, CSV, XML format

Sending reports to employees by email

Automated sending of reports to employees via email from the System using customizable templates

Analytical sections

To get detailed information, you can interactively switch to another information layer, in which the data will be presented in an analytical section. Any accounting data can be used as analytical sections

Accounting of working hours

Keeping a record of the working time by tasks

Keep a record of the time spent on the task (planned and actual). Use the data to analyze the complexity of non-standard tasks

Online timesheet

Recording the working status during the day both manually and by downloading data from the ACS (Access control Systems)

Keeping a timesheet

Forming of the final timesheet, coordination with the direct manager and transfer of data to the personnel system

Distribution of the wage fund for open projects

Keeping a record of the working time by project tasks. Distribution of the wage fund for open projects in proportion to the time used

Integrations and mobile application

KPI Data Collection Robot

Automated by schedule collection of data on quantitative indicators (KPIs) from other accounting systems, including 1C:Enterprise

Two-way integration with payroll system

Implementation of background personnel exchange to maintain an up-to-date organizational structure. Overloading to payroll system of the calculated bonus and personal performance coefficients

Integration with Microsoft Excel

Overloading of planned, actual values and other employee data from Microsoft Excel

Connecting external data sources

The system can receive data from ODBC sources from Microsoft SQL Server, IBM DB2, PostgreSQL, Oracle Database, Microsoft Access, etc.

Mobile application

An application designed to work on mobile devices running iOS and Android
