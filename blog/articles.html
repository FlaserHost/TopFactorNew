Impulse Telecom is one of the leading suppliers of IT equipment and services in Russia, which is in the TOP 100 largest IT companies, as well as in the TOP 25 largest IT providers for telecom operators. When the number of the organization increased to 140 employees, the management of Impulse Telecom decided to implement an automated KPI management system - without this it became impossible to control all work processes in departments.

In order for the implementation to be successful, representatives of Impulse Telecom turned to us for consulting. We analyzed the company's internal business processes and helped develop a strategy.
